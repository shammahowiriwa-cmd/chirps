About CHRIPS

Our Motto

"Educating the Mind and Passionately Watching Them Grow"

This motto encapsulates our dedication to nurturing not just intellectual development, but the holistic growth of every student in our care.

Mission Statement

To make students persevere at hard work with integrity, encouraging solution-seeking attitude and life-long learning that will lead to academic excellence.

Vision Statement

To raise independent and confident learners who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
